Chicken Rice

Chicken Rice is a flavorful dish featuring tender poached or roasted chicken served over fragrant rice cooked in chicken broth. Accompanied by garlic-chili sauce and soy sauce, it's a perfect balance of savory and aromatic flavors. Popular in Southeast Asia, it's both comforting and satisfying, enjoyed by people of all ages. Available in Full Rice, Half Rice, and Small Rice portions.

Mix Rice

Mix Rice is a delicious and wholesome meal combining steamed rice with a variety of curries, vegetables, meats, and sauces. Popular in Sri Lanka and South Asia, it offers a burst of flavors and textures in every bite. It's a filling, customizable dish loved for its rich, spicy taste. Available in Full Rice, Half Rice and Small Rice portions.

Rice And Curry

Rice and Curry is a traditional South Asian meal featuring steamed rice served with a variety of flavorful curries such as Steamed White Rice/Red Rice, chicken curry, fish, dhal, Brinjal Pickle, Chutney, Papadam or vegetables. Rich in spices and aroma, it offers a perfect balance of taste and nutrition, making it a beloved everyday dish in many households.
